About Little Lemon

Founded with a passion for authentic Mediterranean cuisine, Little Lemon brings the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean to Chicago. Our chefs carefully source the finest ingredients to create dishes that honor traditional recipes while embracing modern culinary techniques.

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Our warm, welcoming atmosphere makes every meal a celebration, whether you're joining us for a casual lunch or a special dinner.

Our Values

  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Authentic Mediterranean traditions
  • Exceptional hospitality
  • Community connection
